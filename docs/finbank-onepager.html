Built for

Belgian freelancers with €5k–€25k monthly volume who rely on Wise or Revolut and want a safer fallback when accounts are blocked, closed, or held — especially after large inflows, cross-border payments, or crypto-linked activity.

The issue

When a payment account is blocked:

  • Transfers are stopped and balances can be held for weeks
  • Source-of-funds requests create uncertainty and delays
  • Your business cashflow becomes dependent on a single provider's decision
What FinBank provides

A self-custody treasury application on Base (EVM L2) designed to reduce single-point-of-failure risk.

FinBank cannot freeze or move funds held in your self-custody vault.

Self-custody by default You control the funds in your on-chain vault
Isolated vault per user No pooled balance, full transparency
IBAN bridge for EUR rails Via Monerium (regulated)
If the IBAN is blocked

Monerium controls the IBAN service. Once funds are in your self-custody vault, you can withdraw on-chain immediately to any address or exchange. There is no lockup by FinBank.

How it works
1.Create your account — Face ID via ERC-4337 smart account, no seed phrase required
2.Receive EUR via your Monerium IBAN
3.Hold value as EURe in your isolated vault on Base
4.View account activity — deposits, withdrawals, balances
5.Withdraw anytime — back to IBAN or on-chain exit immediately
